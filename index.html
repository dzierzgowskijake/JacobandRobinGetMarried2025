Our Story

Well, we went to the same college…during COVID. So while we technically shared a school, we never actually shared a classroom—or even a campus. Thanks to remote learning, a portion of our college experience happened entirely from behind a screen.

But fate (and Instagram) had other plans.

Robin had a virtual class with one of Jacob’s best friends. Naturally, like any curious online student, she ended up doing a little social media exploring. That’s when she stumbled across Jacob’s account. She followed. He followed back. And just like that, the story began.

We started talking soon after and quickly became best friends. For almost a full year, we texted, FaceTimed, and talked about anything and everything. Even though we’d never met in person, we somehow felt like we’d known each other forever.

Then finally, we met. No screens, no buffering, just us. And from that very first day, we’ve been inseparable. Nearly every single day since has been spent together.

Four years later, we’re still best friends, still cracking each other up, and still amazed that one random follow led us to exactly where we were meant to be.
